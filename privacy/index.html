Privacy Policy

Overview

Last Updated: February 2026

Sajugazer is committed to respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We designed this service with a privacy-first approach: all Saju chart calculations run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript, and we do not require any form of registration, login, or account creation to use our services.

Information We Collect

Sajugazer does not collect or store personal birth data on any server. All birth date, birth time, gender, and geographic location inputs you provide are processed locally in your browser for the sole purpose of generating your Saju (Four Pillars of Destiny) chart. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We have no server-side database, no user accounts, and no mechanism to access the data you enter.

When you visit our site, standard web server log data may be automatically collected by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages). This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring page, and the date and time of your visit. This information is collected and managed by the hosting provider and is used solely for maintaining site security and understanding aggregate traffic patterns. Sajugazer does not independently collect, process, or store this server log data.

Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses browser local storage for essential site functionality. Specifically, we store the following data locally on your device:

Cookie Consent Preference: A simple flag ("cookieConsent") that records whether you have acknowledged the cookie consent banner. This contains no personal information.

Chart Input Data: Your birth date, birth time, gender, longitude, and reference longitude ("sajuBirthData") are saved in your browser's local storage so that your chart data persists as you navigate between analysis pages (Total, Daily, Monthly, Yearly, Decade). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-party services integrated into this site, such as Google AdSense, may set their own cookies for advertising and service operation purposes. These cookies are governed by the respective third party's privacy policy. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though doing so may affect site functionality or the display of certain content.

How We Use Your Information

The birth data you enter is used exclusively and solely for client-side Saju chart computation and interpretation within your own browser. We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. We have no access to the data you input. Aggregate, non-identifying statistical data from hosting provider logs may be used to understand general traffic patterns and improve the overall user experience of the site.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with your use of Sajugazer. Because all Saju calculations are performed entirely on the client side within your browser, sensitive birth data never leaves your device and is not exposed to network transmission risks. Our site is served over HTTPS to encrypt all communication between your browser and our hosting provider. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data stored in your browser's local storage, particularly on shared or compromised devices.

Third-Party Services

Sajugazer may integrate third-party services for purposes such as hosting, content delivery, and site monetization. These third-party providers may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of any third-party service whose content appears on our site.

If third-party advertising services are present, they may use cookies to serve content based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. You can typically manage your preferences for such services through your browser's cookie settings or through the respective provider's opt-out mechanisms.

Sajugazer does not independently operate any analytics, user tracking, or data collection platform. We do not use Google Analytics or any similar service to monitor individual user behavior.

Your Data Removal Rights

Since all personal data (birth date, time, gender, and location) is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted to any server, you have full control over your data at all times. To permanently remove all stored information, simply clear your browser's local storage or cache for the sajugazer.com domain. You can do this through your browser's settings or developer tools. Once cleared, no trace of your input data remains anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Sajugazer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since our service does not require registration and does not transmit any user-entered data to a server, the risk of inadvertent data collection from minors is minimal. If you believe a child has used our service in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us so we can provide guidance.

Shared & Public Devices

If you access Sajugazer from a shared, public, or borrowed device, we recommend clearing your browser's local storage and cache after use to remove any stored chart input data. This ensures that the next person using the device cannot view your previously entered birth information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, the addition of new features, or applicable regulations. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Consent

By accessing and using Sajugazer, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the service.

Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: sajugazer@gmail.com
