Privacy Policy

Overview

Sajugazer is committed to respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We designed this service with a privacy-first approach: all Saju calculations run entirely in your browser, and we do not require registration or login.

Information We Collect

Sajugazer does not collect or store personal birth data on any server. All birth date, time, and gender inputs you provide are processed locally in your browser for the sole purpose of generating your Saju chart. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

When you visit our site, standard web server log data may be automatically collected by our hosting provider. This may include your IP address, browser type, referring page, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used solely for maintaining site security and understanding aggregate traffic patterns.

Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses cookies and browser local storage for essential site functionality. Specifically, we store your cookie consent preference and your chart input data (such as birth date, time, and region) in your browser's local storage. This data persists until you manually clear your browser data, and is used solely to provide the Saju chart calculation service across pages. It is never transmitted to any server or shared with external parties.

Third-party services integrated into this site may also set their own cookies for service operation purposes. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though doing so may affect site functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used exclusively to operate, maintain, and improve the Sajugazer service. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Aggregate, non-identifying statistical data may be used to analyze site performance and user experience trends.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with your use of Sajugazer. Since all Saju calculations are performed client-side, sensitive birth data never leaves your device. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of server log data.

Third-Party Services

Sajugazer may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or content delivery. These services may collect standard usage data as described in their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of any third-party service you interact with through our site.

Children's Privacy

Sajugazer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Shared & Public Devices

If you access Sajugazer from a shared or public device, we recommend clearing your browser cache and local storage after use to remove any stored session data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Consent

By using Sajugazer, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: sajugazer@gmail.com
