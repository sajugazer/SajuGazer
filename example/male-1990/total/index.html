About Personality

Bright and passionate like the sun, you possess honesty that reveals yourself without concealment and lively sociability. You know how to enjoy life's pleasures without crossing the line, and through diligence and steady movement, you actively create abundance for yourself as a highly productive individual.

You may be weak in fine finishing details or numerical calculations, leaving outcomes slightly incomplete, and you may act based more on principles or emotions than practical gain. Your sense of direction can be somewhat blurred, leading to strong starts but weaker conclusions, yet your pure, uncalculating humanity is a distinct charm. With a clear distinction between public and private matters and a strong sense of justice, you earn the trust of those around you. You are wise enough to pursue both social status and personal happiness at the same time.

You maintain a strong balance between emotion and reason, allowing you to empathize with the pain of others while offering objective advice. You are a person of refined character and dignity.
