About Sajugazer

Who We Are

Sajugazer is an independent web service dedicated to making the ancient art of Saju (Four Pillars of Destiny) accessible to a global audience. We combine thousands of years of Eastern metaphysical tradition with modern algorithmic computation to deliver precise, structured, and insightful Saju chart analyses—all completely free and without requiring any registration.

What is Saju?

Saju (사주), also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a traditional East Asian philosophical system that interprets the patterns of a person's life through the cosmic energy present at the moment of their birth. Based on the year, month, day, and hour of birth, eight characters (known as Saju-palja) are derived using the Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches—a sexagenary cycle that has been used for thousands of years to map the flow of time and energy.

Unlike simple horoscopes, Saju is a deeply layered analytical framework. It examines the interplay of Yin-Yang balance, the Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water), the Ten Stars (Sipseong), Fateful Stars (Sinsal), and the 12 Stages of Energy (Unseong) to build a comprehensive portrait of an individual's personality, strengths, challenges, and life trajectory. Far from fortune-telling, Saju is best understood as a humanistic tool for self-awareness and reflection.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional Eastern philosophy and the modern digital world. Saju has long been a profound tool for self-reflection in East Asian cultures, yet it has remained largely inaccessible to those outside the region due to language barriers and the complexity of manual chart calculation. Sajugazer aims to change that by providing an intuitive, accurate, and educational platform that anyone—regardless of background or prior knowledge—can use to explore the patterns encoded in their birth data.

Three Pillars of Sajugazer

Sajugazer is built around three core pillars that work together to deliver a complete Saju experience:

  • Tool — Precision Chart Calculator: Our engine computes Four Pillars charts from your birth date, time, and geographic location using a sophisticated chronological system that includes solar term calibration, regional longitude correction, and daylight saving time handling. Simply enter your birth data to generate an accurate chart instantly. Your chart includes the full Eight Characters, Hidden Stems, elemental proportions, Yin-Yang balance, and an AI-ready prompt that you can copy and paste into any AI assistant for further personalized analysis.
  • Analysis — Structured Life Readings: Go beyond raw chart data. Sajugazer provides in-depth readings across five fortune dimensions: Total Analysis (a comprehensive personality, success, love, and wealth profile), Daily Fortune (day-by-day energy guidance), Monthly Fortune (month-level trend insights), Yearly Fortune (annual energy forecasts), and Decade Fortune (10-year life cycle analysis based on Daewun). Each reading draws from Ten Stars, Fateful Stars, 12 Stages, Hidden Stems, Yin-Yang balance, and Five Element proportions to deliver a nuanced interpretation.
  • Learn — Educational Content Library: New to Saju? Our library of in-depth articles covers everything from the fundamentals of Four Pillars philosophy to advanced concepts like Sipseong (Ten Stars), Sinsal (Fateful Stars), and the 12 Unseong (Stages of Energy). Each article is designed to help you understand not just your own chart, but the rich intellectual tradition behind it—empowering you to become your own reader over time.

This three-part structure—Tool, Analysis, and Learn—ensures that Sajugazer serves both as a practical utility and as an educational resource, making it a comprehensive destination for anyone interested in the Four Pillars of Destiny.

How It Works

Using Sajugazer is straightforward. Enter your birth year, month, day, hour, and minute, then select your birth city or input your geographic coordinates. Sajugazer uses your longitude to apply a precise solar time correction—because Saju is based on true solar time, not clock time. The engine then calculates your Four Pillars, determines your Day Master, identifies the Ten Stars and Fateful Stars in your chart, and evaluates your Five Element balance.

Once your chart is generated, you can explore five analysis pages: Total, Daily, Monthly, Yearly, and Decade. Each page offers a different temporal lens through which to understand how cosmic energy interacts with your birth chart. Additionally, a one-click AI prompt feature lets you copy a structured summary of your chart data, optimized for use with AI assistants, so you can receive even more personalized interpretations beyond what our built-in analysis provides.

Our Technology

Sajugazer is built on a high-fidelity chronological engine that accounts for solar term boundaries, regional longitude offsets, and the full sexagenary cycle. Unlike simplified calculators, our system handles edge cases such as births near solar term transitions and midnight boundary crossings with precision. The entire computation runs client-side in your browser, ensuring both speed and privacy—no data is ever sent to a server.

Our Philosophy

Sajugazer is designed as a tool for self-reflection and insight, not deterministic prediction. We believe that understanding your innate patterns empowers you to make better decisions—not that your fate is fixed. As the tradition teaches: "Know your destiny (Ji-myeong), and you gain the wisdom to navigate it."

We view Saju not as superstition, but as a structured language for understanding human nature. Each chart is a snapshot of cosmic energy at a specific moment—an invitation to reflect on your strengths, tendencies, and growth areas. Our interpretations draw from classical Myeongrihak scholarship while adapting the language for a contemporary, international audience. We strive for depth without dogma, and clarity without oversimplification.
