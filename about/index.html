About Sajugazer

Who We Are

Sajugazer is an independent web service dedicated to making the ancient art of Saju (Four Pillars of Destiny) accessible to a global audience. We combine thousands of years of Eastern metaphysical tradition with modern algorithmic computation to deliver precise, structured, and insightful Saju chart analyses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional Eastern philosophy and the modern digital world. Saju has long been a profound tool for self-reflection in East Asian cultures, yet it has remained largely inaccessible to those outside the region. Sajugazer aims to change that by providing an intuitive, accurate, and educational platform that anyone can use to explore the patterns encoded in their birth data.

What We Offer

  • Precise Saju Chart Calculation: Our engine computes Four Pillars charts using a sophisticated chronological system, including solar term calibration and regional longitude correction.
  • Comprehensive Analysis: View your Ten Stars, Fateful Stars, 12 Stages, Hidden Stems, Yin-Yang balance, and Five Element proportions at a glance.
  • Fortune Readings: Explore your Daily, Monthly, Yearly, and Decade luck cycles with detailed interpretations.
  • Educational Content: Learn the fundamentals of Saju philosophy through our in-depth articles on core concepts.

Our Approach

Sajugazer is designed as a tool for self-reflection and insight, not deterministic prediction. We believe that understanding your innate patterns empowers you to make better decisionsâ€”not that your fate is fixed. As the tradition teaches: "Know your destiny (Ji-myeong), and you gain the wisdom to navigate it."

Privacy & Trust

We do not store your personal birth data on any server. All calculations are processed locally in your browser, and input data remains strictly on your device. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
